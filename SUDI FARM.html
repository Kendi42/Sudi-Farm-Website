Our Story

We are Kenya's premier suppier of inputs for livestock and crop production empowering millions of farmers in Kenya. Sudi Farm Limited was founded over a hundred years ago by a small co-op of farmers in Nanyuki, Kenya Sudi has walked with Kenya every step of the way, fueling the ambition of the young nation. We grow, because we help you grow.

Mission

To raise a strong and prosperous nation, By promoting a sustainable agricultural practices, a nation must have high quality food to feed its people. Sudi has generations of experience reliably delivering home-grown farm products to the business, homes and eventually the plates of Kenyans.

Vision

A resilient rural Africa where people and the environment thrive.
